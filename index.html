OUR OTHER SERVICES

SPRAY FINISHING

Spraying is a very popular choice everywhere from tables to kitchen fronts. Spraying is a fast, efficient and reliable way to lay down smooth, uniform coating that adhere well, dry predictably and requires minimal further processing.

FIRE PAINTING

Fire retardant paints and intumescent coatings are vital to ensure the safety and fire resistance of timber and many other products. Fire retardant paints offer between 30 and 120 minutes of fire resistance.

FRENCH POLISHING

French Polishing is a wood finishing technique that results in a very high gloss surface, with a deep colour and chatoyancy.

PRODUCTION SPECIFICS

OUR PROCESS OF WORK

The core service is specialist spray painting. Everything has the potential to be sprayed - even plastic cows. They can spray any timber, metal, glass or PVC substrate. Finishes include satin, clear, metallic, paint, staining, two pack painting and polishing. They also provide anti bacterial spray finishes, fire retardant finishes and splash glass. They use traditional and modern lacquers with airless top of the range spray equipment that reduces over spray. In addition, we do bespoke restoration and upholstery of individual pieces. Typical clients are joinery manufacturers, interior designers, shop fitters, sign providers and furniture manufacturers. If you understand the technical specifications this is helpful, but a basic understanding of the process is fine.
